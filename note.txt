Before Going Live
==================

1. Add actual before/after photos to photos/ and update photos/manifest.json
2. Get a free Web3Forms access key at web3forms.com (just enter your email) and replace YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE in contact.html
3. Update the placeholder phone number (0400 000 000) in contact.html
4. Update the placeholder email (info@minsconstructions.com.au) in contact.html
5. Add a logo image to images/ (optional)
6. Add a favicon to images/ (optional)
7. Enable GitHub Pages: repo Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from a branch" → Branch: main / (root)
