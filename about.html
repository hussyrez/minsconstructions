Our Rendering Services

We offer a range of rendering techniques and finishes to suit every style and budget

Smooth Rendering

A sleek, polished finish that gives your home a clean, modern look. Achieved using premium acrylic renders that provide a smoother complexion than traditional options. The base coat is prepared to a high standard to ensure an even, flat finish across the entire surface.

Textured Rendering

Add depth and character to your walls with a range of textured finishes. Choose from sand, sandstone, marble, stone chip, stipple, and more. Available in acrylic-bound designer finishing coats that offer durability and a distinctive look for any property.

Pigmented Finish

Rendering can be natural or coloured through an endless range of pigmented finishes. Our coloured acrylic coatings are mixed to your chosen shade, giving you full control over the final look — no painting required after application.

Membrane Colouring

An energy-efficient membrane coating system that reflects up to 50% of solar heat through infrared reflection. Combines a protective weather barrier with a quality colour finish — helping reduce cooling costs while keeping your home looking great.
